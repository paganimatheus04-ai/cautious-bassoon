# **App Name**: Pagani Solutions

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Display a large industrial image with a title, subtitle, and a 'Solicitar Orçamento' (Request a Quote) button.
- About Us Section: Showcase the company's mission, vision, and values in a three-column layout, accompanied by a brief institutional text highlighting quality, experience, and trust.
- Solutions Section: Present different packaging solutions such as 'Caixas corte e vinco', 'Caixas convencionais', 'Projetos especiais', and 'Desenvolvimento técnico', each displayed in card format with an image.
- Services Section: List available services including 'Produção de caixas', 'Divisórias e acessórios', 'Projetos sob medida', 'Logística personalizada', and 'Entrega just-in-time'.
- Contact Form: Provide a contact form for users to submit their name, company, email, telephone, and message.
- Contact Information: Display contact details such as phone number, email address, and physical address, along with social media icons.
- Fixed Navigation Menu: Implement a fixed navigation menu at the top of the page with links to 'Início', 'Sobre Nós', 'Soluções', 'Serviços', and 'Contato'.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saturated orange (#FF8C00) to reflect energy and innovation in the packaging solutions, fitting the futuristic style.
- Background color: Light gray (#D3D3D3), slightly desaturated (20%) to maintain a modern, corporate look while keeping the design light and clean.
- Accent color: Dark gray (#4A4A4A), contrasting with the primary color to highlight interactive elements and maintain a balanced aesthetic.
- Body and headline font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif, for a contemporary, precise, fashionable feel. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use modern and minimalist icons to represent different services and solutions, ensuring they align with the overall futuristic theme.
- Design a clean, corporate layout with a focus on readability and user experience. Utilize whitespace effectively to create a sense of spaciousness and modernity.
- Incorporate subtle hover and fade-in effects to enhance interactivity and provide a smooth user experience.
